BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index, BMI is an easy way to estimate your total body fat. BMI is dependent on your height and weight and helps to determine if your weight is healthy enough for your height.
The greater your BMI, the more body fat you have. Having a high amount of body fat puts you at risk of health problems such as cancer, diabetes and heart disease.







BMI has its own limitations: the body fat of body-builders with high muscle mass may be over-estimated by BMI and that of older persons may also be under-estimated as they have lost muscle mass, so do well to consult your medical provider.
